# HR Employee Attrition Analysis

## Overview
Analysed the IBM HR dataset of 1,470 employees using MySQL 
and Excel to identify why employees leave the company.

## Dashboard Preview
![Dashboard Preview](Dashboard_Preview.png)

## Tools Used
- **MySQL** — 5 SQL queries for attrition analysis
- **Excel** — 5-chart dashboard visualisation

## Key Findings
- The sales department has the highest attrition at 20.6%
- OverTime employees quit at 3× the rate (30.5% vs 10.4%)
- Low salary band has 27.1% attrition vs 3.4% for Very High
- Employees under 30 leave at nearly double the rate of 40+

## SQL Queries Written
- Attrition rate by Department
- Attrition rate by Age Group
- Attrition rate by Salary Band
- OverTime impact on attrition
- Leavers vs Stayers profile comparison

## Recommendations
1. Reduce mandatory overtime — the biggest single driver of attrition
2. Review salary bands for low-income employees
3. Build retention programs targeting the under-30 age group
4. Focus HR's attention on the Sales department first
